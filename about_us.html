ABOUT US


TecTecCar is a trusted community marketplace that connects drivers with empty seats to Tecnun students looking for a ride. More than 1,000 students use TecTecCar every quarter creating an entirely new, people powered, transport network. With a dedicated customer service, a state of the art web, and a fast-growing community of users, TecTecCar is making travel social, money-saving and more efficient for thousands of members.

It would be a pleasure for us to work on your suggestions: info@tecteccar.es
